The ingredients of cookie dough

Infredients:

1 cup(2 sticks) unsalted butter softened ( you can do this by heating up the butter for a short period amount of time )

3/4 cup granulated sugar

1/2 cup packed light born sugar

1 large egg

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

-2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour

-1/2 cup mini chocolate chips (optional)

- 1/2 cup sprinkles (optional)

Instruction

1. Beat butter and suggar

In a large bowl, use an electric mixer to beat the softened butter on medium speed until creamy. Gradually beat in the granulated sugar and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

2. Add egg and vanilla:

Add the egg and vanilla extract to the creamed mixture and beat until just combined.

3. Gradually add flour:

Reduce mixer speed to low and gradually add the flour, beating until just incorporated. Do not overmix.

4. Add chocolate chips or sprinkles (optional):

If desired, stir in the mini chocolate chips and/or sprinkles.
